Finding engagement Rate

So we need to find the engagement rate, for this I am developing a new variable which takes into analytics, comments, retweet and likes into account assigning weights to each of them,

Comments: 30%

Retweets: 40%

likes: 30%

\[ Engagement = \frac{Likes + Retweets + Comments}{Analytics}\times 100 \]

There is particular trend for ThuggerDaily to tweet before any major even which took place into court the following day or after some few days

We will calculate the overall sentiment using the following formula:

\[ \text{Sentiment Score}_{(Headline, Snippet, Article)} =\\ \begin{cases} 1 \times \text{Sentiment Score} & \text{if Sentiment = 'Positive'} \\-1 \times \text{Sentiment Score} & \text{if Sentiment = 'Negative'}\end{cases} \]

Now we are giving more weight to the sentiment shown in the headlines than the sentiment shown in description or article as most people form opinion about a subject from reading than headlines alone, more over the articles are mostly paywalled in these websites so article are getting the least weight

\[ \text{Overall Sentiment Score} = \frac{0.4\times S_{Headlines}+0.35\times \times S_{Snippets}+0.25\times S_{Article}}{3} \]

\[ \text{Overall Sentiment} = \begin{cases} Positive, \text{ Overall Sentiment Score}\geq0\\ Negative, \text{ Otherwise} \end{cases} \]
